Free coding workshops for youth

Our Dojo sessions welcome anyone 7 - 17 years old (along with their parents) and cover everything from basic HTML & CSS, to JavaScript, game development and web programming.
Most sessions require no prior knowledge!

Rebecca Garcia
Co-Founder

Rebecca is currently the CTO of Greatist.com a health fitness and wellness media startup. Previously she was a developer at Do Something; the largest non-profit getting youth involved in social change. After a summer teaching web development at MIT iD Tech Camps, she loved it so much she wanted to share it with NYC. When not teaching kids to code, she enjoys yoga, baking pie and salsa dancing.

Carl Sednaoui
Co-Founder

Carl is a web developer by passion. He has previously taught basic HTML & CSS classes as well as French and Spanish while living in Beijing. When he's not coding, Carl enjoys eating loads of sushi and listening to live music in Washington Square Park.

Ricky Cheng
Mentor: Senior Sensei

As children experience the web for the first time, often times they are engrossed with feelings of curiosity and excitement. Ricky was no different. At a young age, he spent countless hours scouring the web seeking out resources and guidance with programming. Being fully aware of the struggles of self-education, these days Ricky can be found teaching middle schoolers in underprivileged communities how to code. When he's not busy doing that, he's hacking away as a full stack software engineer for a start up in NYC.
